Cougar

Come hunt trophy cougar on the 2.3 million acre Crow Reservation in Montana. Hunts are conducted from Jan through February. Hunts are 1x1, 7 days in length and there are no observers allowed on the hunt. The hunt includes food, lodging, guaranteed licenses and guide. Clients can use a bow, rifle or handgun on this hunt. Hunts are contracted on an on-call basis and the client has 48 hours or less to report to camp. On these types of hunts, clients have had 100% opportunity on cougars. Over the years the outfitter has taken 2 lions over the Boone & Crockett minimum. Tribal members do not typically hunt cougars, so this is a great hunt to harvest a trophy tom cougar in an underutilized population as this outfitter only takes 2 hunters a year.
