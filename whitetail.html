TEXAS WHITETAIL HUNTING

Texas is home to over 3.6 million whitetail deer and is one of the most sought-after native game species in North America. Through one of our outfitters, we have access to multiple properties here in Texas to provide some of the best hunting opportunities to our clients.

Stinnett, Tx: 10,000 acres (Free Range)

Hunts are all inclusive for 3 1/2 days and 4 nights

· Largest deer we can find on the property. You can expect to see mature 8-10-point bucks on this property $4750

Falfurrias, Tx: 2,200 Acres (Native Whitetail)

Hunts are all inclusive for 2 1/2 days and 3 nights

· Classic Whitetail Hunt (Mature 8/9 pt buck with two does) $3750

· Depending on availability scimitar oryx may be available as an add-on

Hext, Tx: 600 Acres (Native Whitetail)

Hunts are all inclusive for 3 1/2 days and 4 nights meals, lodging, guide, game care, one buck, and one doe.

· Management Hunt= $4000

· 145"-155" = $5250

· 156"- 165" = $6750

· 166"- 175" = $7750

· 176"- 185" = $8750

· 186"- 195" = $11,000

· 196"- 205" = $13,500

Depending on availability axis, blackbuck, addax, or nilgai may be added on.
