Spring Turkey

Come hunt both Merriam and Rio turkeys on the 2.3 million acre Crow Reservation in Montana. Hunts are conducted from April to mid-May and have a two person minimum and six person maximum. There's both a 3 day (2 birds a hunter) and a 4 day (3 birds a hunter) hunt option. Hundreds of turkeys can be seen on the hunt and up to three birds can be taken with either a shotgun or a rifle. The hunt includes lodging, guaranteed licenses and guide. Clients provide their own meals on this hunt. The lodge can accommodate up to 6 hunters and is full stocked with all kitchen ware, bedding and towels. Clients can expect to see birds all day long and this hunt is a great way to knock out two species of birds on the same trip. No age requirement on kids and they just need a valid hunter safety card on their possession.
